Sum Of Natural Numbers - 01 - Problem Statement

1) Description:
You will be given a number 'N' (or) 'num'. Your task is to find Sum of Natural Numbers

2) Specification (I/O Format):

i) Input Format:
A single integer num

ii) Output Format:
Display the integer for the given input

3) Example (Sample Test Cases):

i) Sample Input 1:
 4
ii) Sample Output 1:
 10

i) Sample Input 2:
 10
ii) Sample Output 2:
 55

4) Definition:
Natural Numbers are numbers from 1 to infinity (generally specified number 'N' (or) 'num')
Sum of natural numbers are from 1 to N (i.e.) 1,2,3,...,N
Using Formula: sum = (N*(N+1)) / 2
