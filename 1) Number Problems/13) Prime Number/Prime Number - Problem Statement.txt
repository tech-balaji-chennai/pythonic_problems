Prime Number - Problem Statement

Problem Statement:
You will be given a number 'num'. Your task is to find whether given number is Prime Number Or Not

Example:

input 1: 
 5
output 1: 
 Prime number

input 2: 
 4
output 2: 
 Not Prime number

Definition:
A prime number is a natural number greater than 1 that has no positive divisors other than 1 and itself.
Factors of num = {1, num}, so it is a prime number
