Factor Of A Number - Problem Statement

Problem Statement:
You will be given 2 numbers. Your task is to find whether the given number is Factor of Number

Example:

input 1: 
 10
2
output 1: 
 Yes

input 2: 
 10
3
output 2: 
 No 

Definition:
Factor of a number is a number (divisor) which divides that number (dividend) completely without any remainder
Dividend = (Divisor * Quotient) + Remainder
